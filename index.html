About

Welcome to The Energy Forge, a hub for cutting-edge research in sustainable energy storage solutions. Our mission is to revolutionize energy storage with efficient and eco-friendly technologies.

Projects

Gravity Battery

Our gravity battery system leverages the potential energy of lifted weights to store and release energy efficiently. This method provides long-term storage with minimal environmental impact.

Sand Battery

Our sand battery research focuses on thermal energy storage, where sand is heated and retains energy for extended periods, making it a promising solution for renewable energy integration.
